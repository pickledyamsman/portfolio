About Me

My name is Matthew Cianciolo and I am a web developer that recently moved back to America from South Korea.

I lived in South Korea for five years where I taught English, managed classrooms, and created content for students. In this position I was able to gauge the problems that students had so that I could better tailor the curriculum to their needs.

I recently completed the development bootcamp through Flatiron School where I learned: Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, AngularJS, jQuery, and full stack development among some other languages. With the skills I gained through Flatiron School, I have been able to create applications that have helped me in my everyday life.

My wife will be starting enrollment in the UGA counseling graduate program in the Fall. We have been exploring the city and trying out all of the amazing food that Athens has to offer!
